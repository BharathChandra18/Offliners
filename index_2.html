About us

Our goal is to foster a culture of Sports Clubs in Schools and Colleges by providing access to world-class facilities for young children. Our ultimate aspiration is to see India win Olympic GOLD medals in various sports. To achieve this, we regularly host Bi-Annual Tournaments in sports such as Cricket, Badminton, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, and Kho-Kho. Our flagship events are the Inter-School Summer and Winter Cups. Additionally, we also host Corporate Tournaments.

SUMMER CUP

Usually held in mid June of every year.

WINTER CUP

Usually held in mid November of every year.

Corporate Tournaments

We derive our income solely from corporate tournaments and do not profit from school tournaments.
